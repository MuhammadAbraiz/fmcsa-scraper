Carrier Email Scraper

Enter the MC number range to generate a CSV of carriers with 1 power unit and "authorized for property" status, including each carrier's registered email pulled from FMCSA. The CSV also includes a "Likely Equipment" column (Dry Van / Reefer / Flatbed-Stepdeck / Box Truck) inferred from FMCSA cargo data — this is a best-effort guess for prioritizing calls, not a verified fact. FMCSA has no field for Power Only or Hot Shot, so those aren't distinguishable from the data.
